# NME GYM Legal Documents

This document contains the official Terms and Conditions, Privacy Policy, and Refund Policy for NME GYM.

---

## 1. Terms and Conditions

### Membership Eligibility
*   By registering at NME GYM, you certify that you are in good physical health and have no medical conditions that would prevent you from safely using the gym facilities.
*   Members under the age of 18 must have parental or guardian consent.

### Usage Rules
*   Members must follow the instructions of NME GYM staff and trainers at all times.
*   Appropriate gym attire and clean indoor footwear are mandatory.
*   NME GYM reserves the right to terminate membership without refund for any member found violating safety protocols, engaging in harassment, or damaging equipment.

### Liability Waiver
*   NME GYM is not responsible for any injuries sustained during training or while on the premises.
*   The gym is not liable for the loss or theft of personal belongings. Members are advised not to bring valuables to the facility.

### Membership Renewal
*   Membership plans are valid for the duration specified (Monthly, 3-Months, 6-Months, or Annual).
*   It is the member's responsibility to renew their plan via the Dashboard before the expiry date to avoid service interruption.

## 2. Privacy & Data Collection Policy

NME GYM is committed to protecting your personal data. Here is how we collect and use your information:

### Information We Collect
*   **Identity Data:** Name, Gender, and Date of Birth.
*   **Contact Data:** Phone number and Email address (used for login and notifications).
*   **Payment Data:** Screenshots of UPI/Bank transfer payments (stored securely via Cloudinary).
*   **Activity Data:** Membership status, plan type, and attendance history.

### Purpose of Collection
*   To verify payments and issue Member IDs.
*   To facilitate secure login and dashboard access.
*   To send automated notifications (Emails/WhatsApp) regarding membership status and renewals.
*   To manage facility access and internal record-keeping.

### Data Security
*   All payment proof screenshots are stored on encrypted Cloudinary servers.
*   Personal credentials and passwords are encrypted using high-standard hashing algorithms.
*   We do not sell or share your data with third-party marketing companies.

## 3. Refund and Cancellation Policy

### No-Refund Policy
*   All payments made to NME GYM for membership plans, admission fees, or personal training are **non-refundable**.
*   Once a payment screenshot is submitted and verified, the membership is considered active for the full duration of the selected plan.

### Membership Transfer
*   Memberships are non-transferable between individuals.
*   In exceptional circumstances (e.g., medical reasons supported by a doctor's certificate), NME GYM management may allow a temporary membership freeze, at its sole discretion.

### Verification Failures
*   If a payment submission is rejected due to invalid screenshots or incomplete transfers, the user will be notified. No Member ID will be issued until a valid payment is confirmed.
